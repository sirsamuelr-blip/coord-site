Coord Privacy Policy

Effective date: [MARCH 9, 2026]

Coord (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a mobile app for coordinating group trips. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices users have.

Information We Collect

  • Account and profile information: first name, last name, email address, and account identifier.
  • Trip coordination data: trip titles, destinations, trip dates, requirements, due dates, membership-related data, status updates, and optional notes that users enter in the app.
  • Notification-related data: push notification token(s) used to send app notifications.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To support trip planning and coordination features
  • To manage trip membership and requirement status
  • To send app notifications related to trip coordination and reminders
  • To support account deletion requests and related account management functions

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We use service providers and infrastructure providers to operate the app, such as authentication, cloud database, cloud functions, and push notification services.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app and its features, unless a user deletes their account or applicable law requires otherwise.

Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account from within the app. Users may also visit https://delete.coord-app.com for deletion-related information.

In some cases, deletion may be temporarily blocked if the account is the shared owner of an active trip and ownership transfer is not supported in V1.

Children

Coord is not directed to children.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: [support@coord-app.com]
